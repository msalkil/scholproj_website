How to innovate responsibly while focusing on sustainable technological developments

When I started my project, I was uncertain on what direction I wanted to go with. Anytime I thought of an idea, I didn’t feel too passionate about it or didn't know how to expand it long enough for it to be a great presentation. At the start of the year, I did start to see a lot of media about how AI and other technological factors have been harming the world. Seeing how not only the planet, but the animals were being negatively affected, made me open my eyes. This led me to become more eco-friendly and even start to look at organizations to donate to. Ultimately this is what led me to come up with my idea for my essential question. We use technology on a daily basis, to do homework, watch a movie, and to do our jobs. Before this, I never thought about how technology has an effect on our world besides development. There are many factors that play into this, from the way the materials are sourced, to how we overconsume technology. It is negatively impacting our planet and ultimately ourselves. This is where I got my essential question: “How to innovate responsibly while focusing on sustainable technological developments.”
