Mentorship

My mentorship at Bureau University with William Forrester, in the media department. I have learned a lot. Overall, what we have been learning is how to code HTML, CSS, And JavaScript. This has been a very fun learning experience for me. I loved learning about websites and other media I can create. With the information I have learned so far, I made the coding for my website. My mentor has been a great help to me when I get stuck in my coding. He would walk me through the process and show me how to fix the errors. Not only did I learn to code, but I also helped around the office. Some examples are packing merch, and helping with creating/participating in advertising media, for the rest of the departments. I also attend meetings with other departments, and internal meetings. All of these experiences have helped me better understand how a workplace works. Not only did I learn how to code, but I learned how to interact with coworkers and other departments. For a workplace to function properly, you need to help each other out. In my opinion my mentor's workplace is very healthy. There are tight knitted group, that help each other out whenever it is needed. Everyone was very kind to me and welcoming. They made this a very pleasurable experience for me. I hope whenever I go into the workforce, I can have coworkers and a work environment just as healthy as them.
