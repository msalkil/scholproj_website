William Forrester

~How has AI helped you?~

“It has helped me solve problems that I can't really solve myself. Like if I hit a road block on a project and I have tried everything, that is when I will ask AI”

~How has AI affected the people around you?~

“It hasn't really affected the people around me, it's more of like a tool. It's helped some people, but I wouldn't say it has had a very big impact on anyone around me yet.”

~What are your thoughts on how AI has affected the work industry?~

“They don't need to replace people with ai, ai can always make mistakes. You always need to have people look over what ai has created. They also need to stop putting AI into everything that doesn't need it.”

~How do you predict AI will be in the future(advancements)(next 10 years)?~

“It depends on how we as a society put restrictions and laws on
it. Right now there are not many restrictions or laws so it can go
anyways. First we need to put some restrictions on it”

~What are your thoughts on how AI affects the younger generation?~

“Ai is teaching the younger generations to not really try hard at something. A lot of these younger generations are just plugging stuff into air and copying and pasting, not putting any work into it.”

~What are your major concerns with AI?~

“That it is unrestricted, there needs to be more laws and restrictions.”

~What are some positive things you have seen AI do on a big scale?~

(couldn't think of any big ones, so we did a smaller scale.) “Has helped speed up mundane tasks. I use it to combine two spreadsheets. It is useful for repetitive tasks” (Bonus question, something negative.) “AI art has hurt the art industry.”

~Do you believe AI should have more/stricter regulations?~

“Yes”

~Do you believe AI is harmful to people and the planet?~

“Yes, it cost too much for what it's worth. It takes too much water to cool, and it is eating up a lot of computer memory. This is why “ram” prices are so high right now.”

~What are ways you think AI can be improved?~

“Needs more restrictions, and laws.”
